Research aboard the ISS

At any given time on board the space station, a large array of different experiments are underway within a wide range of disciplines. These experiments are selected by each space station partner to meet the goals of each respective agency. Here, you can search the Space Station Research Explorer database of experiments to learn more about each experiment’s objectives, descriptions, and results, as well as on-board facilities, imagery and additional information that may be found beyond this database.

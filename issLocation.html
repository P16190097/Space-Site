About the tracker

The ISS tracker above tracks the current position of the International Space Station in orbit over the earth, detailing its endless voyage around the world, In real time!

Did you know?

  • The ISS travels at over 17,150 miles per hour on its journey around the globe, thats about 5 miles per second!
  • It takes around 92 minutes for the International Space Station to complete 1 full orbit of the globe.
  • The ISS orbits the earth at an altitude of 408 km above the earths surface in LEO (Low Earth Orbit).
